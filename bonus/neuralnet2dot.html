NAME

neuralnet2dot.pl - Convert neural nets from tinyai to graphviz

SYNOPSIS

neuralnet2dot.pl [options] [input neural network] [output graphviz file]

OPTIONS

--prune-useless, -p

Hide nodes that don't affect the outputs. The program never removes Input, Output, or Bias nodes.
